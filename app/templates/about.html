About Us

Welcome to the Sea Quail database project! This product is dedicated to providing a quality interface for users to interact with the data in the 2023 Lahman Database.

Features

League Standings

Provide us with a league and a year and we'll give you the rankings grouped by division (if appplicable). You can also click on team names here to get to their pages.

Team Summary

Provide us with a team name and a year and we'll give you a list of pitching and batting stats along with a depth chart! Click on a teams name in their summary to get historical info about them! Clicking on a players name will pull up their info.

Immaculate Grid Solver

Enter the two critera from the Immaculate Grid to get a player that matches! We support any combination of two past Immaculate Grid questions. Clicking on a player will take you to their player page where you can learn more about them.
